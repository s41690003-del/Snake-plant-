🌿 Snake Plant

Official Botanical Information

Common Name

Snake Plant (Mother-in-Law's Tongue)

Scientific Details
Botanical Name Dracaena trifasciata
Family Asparagaceae
Plant Type Perennial Succulent
Native Region West Africa
Economic Importance
  • Popular ornamental plant used in homes, schools, offices, hospitals, and public landscapes.
  • Requires very little water and maintenance, making it highly economical for indoor decoration and landscaping.
  • Commercially propagated and sold through nurseries, contributing significantly to the horticulture industry.
  • Helps improve indoor air quality by absorbing certain airborne pollutants.
  • Strong leaf fibres have traditionally been used for making ropes, mats, baskets, and handicrafts.
  • Enhances the aesthetic value of buildings while supporting the ornamental plant trade.
