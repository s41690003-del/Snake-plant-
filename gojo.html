🌿 Snake Plant Information

Common Name

Snake Plant (Mother-in-Law's Tongue)

Botanical Name

Dracaena trifasciata
(Formerly Sansevieria trifasciata)

Economic Importance

  • Widely cultivated as an ornamental plant for homes, offices, schools, and public landscapes.
  • Known for improving indoor air quality by helping remove certain airborne pollutants.
  • Requires minimal water and maintenance, making it a cost-effective landscaping and indoor decoration plant.
  • Commercially propagated and sold through nurseries, contributing to the horticulture industry.
  • Its strong leaf fibres have traditionally been used for making ropes, mats, baskets, and handicrafts.
  • Enhances aesthetic value while supporting the ornamental plant trade.
